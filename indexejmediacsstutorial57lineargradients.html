Default moment linear gradient from top to bottom. To move from left to right, add "to right" before the colors; e.g., background: linear-gradient(to right, blue, yellow). From right to left, add "to left". Angle? Add "to top right". Custom angles? Add "30deg".

Can do three colors. e.g. background: linear-gradient(30deg, green, blue, yellow)

Assign percentage color. e.g. background: linear-gradient(blue, yellow 20%) means from top to bottom, at 20% going down, begin yellow.

No gradient effect. e.g. background: linear-gradient(blue 50%, yellow 50%) assign equal percentage to colors totaling 100%
